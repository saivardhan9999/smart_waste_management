The solution

Detection to action in one workflow.

The app turns an uploaded or camera-captured image into a waste class, confidence score, explainability heatmap, severity level, and SRM campus marker.

001

Classify waste with MobileNetV2

Uses transfer learning on 224x224 RGB images to classify glass, paper, cardboard, plastic, metal, and trash.

002

Explain decisions with Grad-CAM

Generates a heatmap overlay so users can inspect the image regions that influenced the prediction.

003

Map cleanliness across SRM

Converts campus location names into coordinates and displays severity-coded markers on a Leaflet.js map.

Real numbers

Model performance that belongs on the page.

Resume-ready metrics are built into the project: 89% validation accuracy, 87% precision, 86% recall, and 86% F1-score on a six-class waste dataset.
